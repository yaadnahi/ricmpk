ANNUAL CONVENTION

Our ministry organizes yearly convention, an event where we invite notable pastors from major cities of Pakistan to preach the Word of God. The two day event is comprised of prayer and worship. Hundreds of people show up at the event and witness the Holy Spirit. Many people surrender themselves and give their lives to the Lord.

Every year we prepare people for baptism, we teach them the biblical concepts, give them literature, so that they are ready to testify the start of their new life with Christ to the world.

MEET THE FOUNDERS

TARIQ PAUL
Pastor Tariq Paul, is an eloquent speaker of God. Although born and raised in a Christian home, he didn't knew much about his faith until in his early twenties Lord showed him the way to eternal life. From that point on, he gave his life to the Savior Lord Jesus Christ, went to ZBI(Zarephath Bible Institute) and learned Christian Theology with compassion. He then got affiliated with FGA (Full Gospel Assemblies of Pakistan) and did a lot of work to preach the Gospel in the remote areas of Pakistan. In 2008, laid the foundation of FGA Church in Bahawalpur, Pakistan. More recently, God provoked him to expand the boundaries of preaching the Gospel, which gave birth to RICM (Reborn In Christ Ministries) Pakistan. The ultimate goal is to preach the Gospel in unconventional ways, to reach many people so that they repent and are saved in Christ. Include prominent works.
SHAGUFTA PAUL
Pastor Shagufta Paul, is a practicing nurse and leads the women ministry. Born in a Catholic home, she was ingrained with the Catholic traditions. She was a bright student and always had a keen interest in learning new stuff. She always kept herself in the light of the Bible, that is where God showed her the true way, the only way to salvation. She continued her studies and learned Christian Theology at Zarephath Bible Institute(ZBI). Her primary focus in the ministry is to empower women in their professional and household lives as well as in Christ. She strongly believes the fact that women who follow Christ are more content and give rise to a God-centered family. May the Lord use her to the fullest of His needs.

ABOUT PAKISTAN

Since its independence in 1947, Pakistan is home to many people with varying cultural, ethnic, religious backgrounds. A country whose major revenue generator is the agriculturual industry. The fact that it is the fifth most populous country in the world makes life tougher than ever. With scarce resources, it is becoming increasingly challenging to cope with the issues that arise day after day.

Pakistan is gifted with many natural resources, which include many precious stones and minerals. It's strategic location also gives the country many advantages. Scenic landscapes in the northern areas increase its beauty by a thousand times. It is also home to many historical landmarks left by the Mughal and the British Empires. Despite the fact the over the past decades Pakistan has received notoriety for safety concerns but still its tourism flourishes.

Christians make up 1.6% of the total population. Our ministry focuses to make their lives a little better. Although we have to go through a lot of trouble to work for them. God has given us the responsibility for this overwhelming task, and we believe that He will help us to spread the Gospel.

May the Lord shower His blessings on Pakistan. May this nation prosper over the coming years and forevermore.
